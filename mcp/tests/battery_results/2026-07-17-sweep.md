## Knee

Decision rule (brief): among **dedup=on** combos, smallest `edge_budget`/`top_k` whose retention is within 5% of the plateau AND whose worst-case size ≤ 9000 est-tokens (≥25% headroom).

**Finding 1 — ceiling saturated; the headroom clause is inapplicable.** Only **0/15** dedup=on combos land ≤ 9000 est-tokens; every combo's worst-case query sits at ~11999 (≈ the 12000 ceiling). Real RAG chunks (~2k tokens each) plus rich edges (~260 tokens each) saturate the ceiling, and the trim fills to just under it regardless of params — so ≥25% headroom is unmeetable at **any** grid point and cannot discriminate. Headroom is a *ceiling* / chunk-size lever (`EMBEDDINGTON_MAX_RESPONSE_TOKENS`, source_quote/text length), not an `edge_budget`/`top_k` one. The knee is therefore taken on retention + actual edge delivery, holding `top_k` at the shipped 5 (a RAG-breadth lever the KG-only retention metric must not tune).

**edge_budget curve at top_k=5 (dedup=on):**

| edge_budget | mean_ret | mean_returned | mean_pp | worst_tokens |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 20 | 0.118 | 19.5 | 0.985 | 11994 |
| 40 ⭐ | 0.191 | 24.4 | 0.970 | 11997 |
| 60 | 0.191 | 19.6 | 0.939 | 11994 |
| 80 | 0.155 | 15.2 | 0.912 | 11998 |
| 120 | 0.127 | 8.6 | 0.553 | 11955 |

**Finding 2 — edge_budget inverts past the ceiling.** Edges actually delivered peak at **edge_budget=40** and then *fall* as the budget grows (predicate recall falls with them) because a larger request overshoots the ceiling and the trim floors concepts toward the 3-edge floor. Retention at 40 and 60 is tied; 40 delivers more edges and more predicate diversity. Asking for MORE edges past ~40–60 returns FEWER — the high end of the 1–200 `edge_budget` range is a footgun.

**Chosen defaults: `edge_budget=40`, `top_k=5`** (dedup=on). Retention 0.191 (plateau 0.191 at top_k=5, ≥ 0.181 threshold), predicate recall 0.970, 24.4 edges delivered — the edge_budget that maximizes delivered edges / predicate diversity at plateau retention, just below the flooring inversion.

**Differs from the shipped (60, 5)** → `edge_budget` default updated **60→40** in server.py, enrich.py, RESPONSE_SHAPES.md, CHANGELOG.md. `top_k` stays 5.

For maximal KG grounding a caller can pass `top_k=3`, which lifts retention to the full-grid peak **0.273** at `edge_budget=60` (fewer vector chunks cede more of the shared ceiling to KG edges). Documented as caller guidance, not a default change — top_k governs the vector half, which the KG-only retention metric does not score.
